---
name: research-topic
description: Research a topic on the web in the background and report back.
mode: agent
---
Research the topic the user asked about. Search the web, read the most credible sources,
and produce a short spoken-style summary (3-4 sentences, no lists or URLs) covering the key
facts and anything time-sensitive. If the request is ambiguous, pick the most likely
interpretation and say which one you chose.
